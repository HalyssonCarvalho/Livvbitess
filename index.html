Our Story

Born from a Kitchen, Raised with Intention

Livv Bites started in a home kitchen in South Florida — a response to a simple frustration: why did every "healthy" snack taste like cardboard, and every delicious snack come loaded with ingredients you couldn't pronounce?

We set out to change that. Every recipe was tested dozens of times, with a commitment to keeping it clean: real ingredients, honest flavors, and nothing you wouldn't already have in your pantry.

Today, we still make every batch by hand. Because food made with care tastes like it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Livv Bites truly gluten-free? +
Yes! All Livv Bites are made with certified gluten-free ingredients and produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.
Do you ship nationwide? +
We ship to all 50 US states. Orders over $45 ship free. Expect delivery within 3–5 business days.
How long do they stay fresh? +
Livv Bites stay fresh for 14 days at room temperature, or up to 60 days in the refrigerator. Each order ships with a freshness guarantee.
Are any flavors vegan? +
Yes — the Lemon Zest and Rose & Cardamom bites are 100% vegan. The Almond Butter bites have a vegan option available at checkout.
Can I return or exchange my order? +
Because our products are perishable, we don't accept returns. But if something arrives damaged or isn't right, reach out and we'll make it right — always.
